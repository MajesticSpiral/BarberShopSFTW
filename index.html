About

S.F.T.W. was established in 1998 at 3 Abbey Road, Llangollen, then after expansion grew to incorporate ladies' hairdressing. After 10 years of growth the ladies hair & beauty remained at the original premises and S.F.T.W. moved to larger premises next door at 1 Abbey Road. Situated on the banks of the River Dee over looking the steam trains and main Castle Street, S.F.T.W. offers quality men's grooming in a relaxed atmosphere, using products and equipment that compliment men's hair and style.
